About Us

Welcome to Mangata & Gallo

Mangata and Gallo is a premier jewelry store specializing in creating exquisite pieces for special occasions such as engagements, weddings, and anniversaries. Our company primarily operates online but also has a charming storefront located in Austin, Texas, complete with an atelier for a unique browsing experience.

Our Story

Founded by Mariana, a talented Austin local renowned for her beautiful jewelry designs, Mangata & Gallo has grown into a symbol of high-quality and classic elegance. Mariana, who graduated from design school with a specialization in diamond cutting and metal smithing, opened our store in her hometown and expanded our presence online.

Our Craftsmanship

At Mangata & Gallo, we take pride in our meticulous craftsmanship. Mariana, along with her team of skilled artisans, designs and crafts each piece of jewelry with precision and care. Our collection includes a variety of stunning pieces that cater to different tastes and preferences, ensuring that every customer finds something they love.

Our Services

  • Storefront & Atelier: Visit our Austin store to experience our jewelry collection in person and see where the magic happens in our attached atelier.
  • Online Store: Browse and shop our exquisite selection of jewelry online from the comfort of your home.
  • Custom Designs: Work with Mariana and our team to create custom jewelry pieces that reflect your personal style and special moments.
  • Jewelry Repairs: We offer professional repair services to keep your precious jewelry in pristine condition.
